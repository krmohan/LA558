Title: Mapping the Relationship Between Seasonal Floods and Access to Water in Dhaka, Bangladesh

Overview: Using flood maps and interactive maps provided by The World Bank, markers were used to display the key drainage and water access points along the flood zone in the City of Dhaka. Most of the data were acquired as SHP files and converted to geoJSON data before use.

Abstract: Bangladesh experiences seasonal floods every year. Lack of access to water following flood events can be devastating to the people living in Dhaka. This project will focus on visualizing the data provided by the World Bank and Flood Forecasting Warning Centre (BWDB) to identify water access points close to the flood zone. The final product will be a leaflet map of Dhaka with multiple background layers. The 2017 flood map will be downloaded as SHP file and converted to geoJSON. Data from the World Bank will also be converted to geoJSON by using ogre.adc4gis.com/ and geojson.io/. the geoJSON data will be imported to my github repository and pulled into the map from there.
